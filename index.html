About

Squid Game is a South Korean survival thriller series created by Hwang Dong-hyuk and released on Netflix. The series quickly became a global sensation, captivating audiences with its gripping narrative, thought-provoking themes, and intense visuals. The story follows a group of 456 financially struggling individuals who are invited to participate in a mysterious competition.
